"Airports are a long-running game of hide-and-seek between smugglers and customs."

  • Creator: Neeraj Pandey
  • Original Air Date: January 14, 2026
  • Genre: Crime / Thriller / Drama
  • Seasons: 1 Season (7 Episodes)

Synopsis

Set within the high-pressure environment of the Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj International Airport in Mumbai, the series follows Superintendent Arjun Meena and an elite team of customs officers. Tasked by the Finance Ministry to clean up internal corruption and dismantle a global syndicate, they engage in a deadly cat-and-mouse game with the notorious kingpin Bada Chaudhary. The story explores the intricate world of international smuggling corridors spanning from Bangkok to Milan.
