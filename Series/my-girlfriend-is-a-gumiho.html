  • IMDb Rating: 8.2/10

Synopsis

Cha Dae-woong is an aspiring action star who accidentally releases a Gumiho—a legendary nine-tailed fox—who was trapped in a painting for 500 years. After she saves his life by giving him her mystical "fox bead," the two become inseparable. While Dae-woong is initially terrified she might eat his liver, he eventually falls for the quirky, meat-loving fox as she tries to navigate the modern world and fulfill her dream of becoming a real human.
