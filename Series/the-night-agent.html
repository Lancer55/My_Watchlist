Synopsis

The Night Agent follows FBI agent Peter Sutherland, who is thrown into a vast conspiracy about a mole at the highest levels of the United States government. To save the nation, he plunges into a desperate hunt for the traitor while protecting a former tech CEO from the assassins who murdered her family.
