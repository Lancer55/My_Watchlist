Synopsis

Set in the X-Men universe, an ordinary suburban family's life is turned upside down when they discover their children possess mutant powers. Forced to go on the run from a hostile government agency known as Sentinel Services, the family joins an underground network of mutants fighting for survival and civil rights in a world that fears and hates them.
