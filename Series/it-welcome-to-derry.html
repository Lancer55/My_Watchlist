Synopsis

IT: Welcome to Derry is a 2025 horror series that serves as a prequel to the "IT" films. Set in the 1960s, it explores the origin story of Pennywise the Clown and the dark history of the town of Derry, Maine. As a group of local residents begins to disappear, the terrifying truth about the ancient evil lurking beneath the town begins to surface.
