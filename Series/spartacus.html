Synopsis

The epic saga of the legendary Thracian gladiator who led a massive slave rebellion against the Roman Republic. Spanning from his rise in the Ludus of Batiatus to the final war against Crassus, the story continues with the "House of Ashur," exploring an alternate history where the cunning manipulator Ashur survived Vesuvius and was gifted a gladiator school of his own.
