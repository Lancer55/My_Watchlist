Synopsis

Haunted by the guilt of her past, Mumbai's newly-appointed DCP Rita Ferreira must embark on an investigation that puts her on a collision course with a cold-blooded killer, even as she has to save her life from falling apart. Based on Vish Dhamija's novel Bhendi Bazaar, the series explores inherited trauma and the dark underbelly of Mumbai.
