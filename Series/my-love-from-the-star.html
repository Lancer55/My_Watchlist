  • IMDb Rating: 8.2/10

Synopsis

Do Min-joon is an alien who landed on Earth 400 years ago during the Joseon Dynasty period. He possesses a near-perfect appearance and enhanced physical abilities involving his vision, hearing, and speed. Having maintained a cynical view of human beings, he finally has only three months left on Earth. However, he unexpectedly falls for Cheon Song-yi, a famous and blunt Hallyu actress who becomes his neighbor.
