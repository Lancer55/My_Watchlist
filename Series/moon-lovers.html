Synopsis

During a total solar eclipse, a 25-year-old 21st-century woman, Go Ha-jin, is transported back in time to the Goryeo Dynasty. She wakes up in the body of Hae Soo and finds herself trapped in the middle of a struggle for power among the many princes of the ruling Wang family. While she initially falls for the gentle 8th Prince, she later develops a deep and tragic bond with the feared 4th Prince, Wang So, who hides his face behind a mask.
