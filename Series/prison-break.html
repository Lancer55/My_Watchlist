Prison Break

"Be the change you want to see in the world."

Series Info

  • Creator: Paul Scheuring
  • Original Air Date: 2005 - 2017
  • Genre: Action / Crime / Drama
  • Seasons: 5 Seasons

Synopsis

An innocent man is sent to death row due to a political conspiracy. His only hope is his brother, Michael Scofield, who makes it his mission to deliberately get sent to the same prison in order to break the both of them out from the inside.
