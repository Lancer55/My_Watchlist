Synopsis

By day, Dexter Morgan is a blood-spatter analyst for the Miami Police Department. By night, he is a serial killer who targets other murderers who have managed to escape the justice system. Guided by his adoptive father's "Code," Dexter balances his dark urges with his life as a brother, boyfriend, and forensic expert.
