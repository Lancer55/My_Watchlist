Synopsis

Breathe is an Indian crime drama series that explores the lives of ordinary men faced with extraordinary circumstances. Kabir Sawant, a brilliant but unconventional officer of the Crime Branch, assembles the pieces of seemingly unconnected deaths that lead to an unlikely suspect—Danny Mascarenhas. Danny is a loving father who will do anything to save his son Josh, who needs an urgent organ transplant.
