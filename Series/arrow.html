Synopsis

After being marooned on a remote island for five years, billionaire playboy Oliver Queen returns to Starling City with a mysterious agenda and a lethal set of new skills. Taking on the persona of the Arrow, a hooded vigilante, he fights to clean up his city and right the wrongs of his family while balancing his secret life with his relationships.
