Synopsis

Set in Renaissance Florence, the series follows the "untold" story of Leonardo da Vinci's early life. A visionary, artist, inventor, and genius, the young Leonardo struggles with his inner demons and a quest for forbidden knowledge. He finds himself caught in the middle of a power struggle between the Medici family and the Vatican while searching for the legendary Book of Leaves.
