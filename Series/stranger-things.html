Synopsis

When a young boy disappears in a small Indiana town, his friends, family, and local police are drawn into an extraordinary mystery involving top-secret government experiments, terrifying supernatural forces, and one very strange little girl. As they search for answers, they uncover a parallel dimension known as the Upside Down.
