Game of Thrones

"When you play the game of thrones, you win or you die."

Series Info

  • Creator: David Benioff, D.B. Weiss
  • Original Air Date: 2011 - 2019
  • Genre: Action / Adventure / Drama / Fantasy
  • Seasons: 8 Seasons

Synopsis

Nine noble families fight for control over the lands of Westeros, while an ancient enemy returns after being dormant for millennia. In a world where politics are as deadly as the blade, the struggle for the Iron Throne leads to shifting alliances, betrayal, and the ultimate battle between the living and the dead.
