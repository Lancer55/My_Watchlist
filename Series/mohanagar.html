Synopsis

A powerful and gripping thriller set within the confines of a police station in Dhaka. Over the course of one intense night, a mix of criminals, businessmen, and police officers find their fates intertwined. OC Harun, a cunning and experienced officer, navigates the murky waters of corruption and justice as he tries to solve a high-stakes case.
