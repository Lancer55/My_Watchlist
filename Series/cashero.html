Synopsis

Kang Sang-woong is an ordinary civil servant who suddenly gains superhuman strength. However, there is a unique catch: his powers are directly tied to the amount of physical cash he carries, and every heroic act drains his bank account. Alongside a group of misfit heroes with equally bizarre power requirements, he must protect the city while balancing his financial survival and fighting a mysterious organization targeting superhumans.
