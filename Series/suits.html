Synopsis

While running from a drug deal gone bad, Mike Ross, a brilliant young college dropout, slips into a job interview with one of New York City's best legal closers, Harvey Specter. Despite not having a law degree, Mike's photographic memory and raw talent impress Harvey, leading the duo to handle high-stakes cases while keeping Mike's secret safe.
