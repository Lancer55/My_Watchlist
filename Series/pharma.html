Synopsis

Pharma follows the journey of K.P. Vinod (played by Nivin Pauly), a middle-class man who enters the high-stakes world of pharmaceutical marketing. As he climbs the corporate ladder, he uncovers a dark underbelly of unethical practices and corruption. The series highlights his moral struggle as he decides whether to protect his career or the lives of innocent people.
