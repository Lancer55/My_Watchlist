Breaking Bad

"I am the one who knocks!"

Series Info

  • Creator: Vince Gilligan
  • Original Air Date: 2008 - 2013
  • Genre: Crime / Drama / Thriller
  • Seasons: 5 Seasons

Synopsis

Walter White, a chemistry teacher diagnosed with inoperable lung cancer, turns to manufacturing and selling methamphetamine to secure his family's future. Partnering with a former student, Jesse Pinkman, he descends into a world of drugs and crime, transforming from a mild-mannered family man into a ruthless kingpin.
