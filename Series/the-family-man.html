Synopsis

Srikant Tiwari is a middle-class man who secretly works as an intelligence officer for the Threat Analysis and Surveillance Cell (TASC), a branch of the National Investigation Agency. He must juggle his demanding, low-paying, high-pressure job with his domestic life, all while protecting the country from terrorists.
