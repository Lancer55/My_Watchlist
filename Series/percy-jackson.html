Synopsis

Demigod Percy Jackson leads a quest across America to prevent a war among the Olympian gods. After discovering his heritage as the son of Poseidon, Percy must find Zeus's stolen master bolt and clear his father's name while navigating a world filled with mythological monsters and meddling deities.
