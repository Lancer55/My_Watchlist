Synopsis

A beautiful love story between Captain Yoo Shi-jin, a special forces officer in the South Korean Army, and Doctor Kang Mo-yeon, a surgeon. Their paths cross in a war-torn country where they work together on peacekeeping and humanitarian missions. Despite the dangers of their professions and their different views on life and death, they find themselves falling deeply in love.
