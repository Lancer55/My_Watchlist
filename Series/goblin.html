Synopsis

Kim Shin is an immortal goblin and protector of souls who has lived for 900 years. He is tired of his immortality and seeks his human bride, the only person who can pull out the sword pierced through his chest and end his life. His life becomes complicated when he meets Ji Eun-tak, a cheerful high school student who claims to be the Goblin's bride, and a Grim Reaper who has no memory of his past life.
