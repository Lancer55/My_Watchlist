Kill Boksoon

"Killing is easier than raising a child."

Movie Info

  • Director: Byun Sung-hyun
  • Release Year: 2023
  • Genre: Action / Crime / Thriller
  • IMDb Rating: 6.6/10

Synopsis

At work, Gil Bok-soon is a renowned professional killer who never misses a target. At home, she is a single mother struggling to connect with her teenage daughter. As she nears the end of her contract, Bok-soon finds herself caught in a kill-or-be-killed situation when she refuses to complete a mission, turning her entire company and the industry's best assassins against her.
