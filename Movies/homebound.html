Homebound

"A quest for dignity in a world of desperation."

Movie Info

  • Director: Neeraj Ghaywan
  • Release Year: 2025
  • Genre: Drama / Social Commentary
  • IMDb Rating: 8.1/10

Synopsis

Two childhood friends from a small North Indian village chase a police job that promises them the dignity they've long been denied. However, as they inch closer to their dream, mounting desperation and the harsh realities of systemic inequality threaten the lifelong bond that holds them together.
