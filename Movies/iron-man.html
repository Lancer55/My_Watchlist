  • Release Year: 2008
  • Genre: Action / Sci-Fi / Adventure

Synopsis

After being held captive in an Afghan cave, billionaire engineer Tony Stark creates a unique weaponized suit of armor to fight evil. Returning to America, he refines the suit and uses it to combat crime and terrorism as the armored superhero known as Iron Man, while dealing with threats from within his own company.
