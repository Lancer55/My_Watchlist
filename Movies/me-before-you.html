Movie Info

  • Director: Thea Sharrock
  • Release Year: 2016
  • Genre: Romance / Drama
  • IMDb Rating: 7.4/10

Synopsis

Louisa Clark is a quirky, happy-go-lucky woman who takes a job as a caregiver for Will Traynor, a wealthy young banker whose life changed dramatically after a tragic accident. As they spend time together, Louisa's sunny outlook challenges Will's cynical worldview, and a deep bond forms that changes both of their lives in ways they never imagined.
