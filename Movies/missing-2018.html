Synopsis

Missing is a 2018 Indian Hindi-language psychological thriller film written and directed by Mukul Abhyankar. The story follows Sushant Dubey and Aparna, who check into a resort in Mauritius with their daughter Titli. Their world turns upside down when Titli goes missing, leading to a tense investigation where the truth is buried under layers of deception.
