Synopsis

Highway is a 2022 Indian Telugu-language mystery crime thriller film written and directed by K. V. Guhan and produced by Venkat Talari through Sree Iswarya Lakshmi Movies and Northstar Entertainment. The story follows a photographer on a road trip who inadvertently crosses paths with a serial killer, leading to a high-stakes game of survival on the open road.
