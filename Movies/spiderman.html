Spider-Man

"With great power comes great responsibility."

Movie Info

  • Director: Sam Raimi
  • Release Year: 2002
  • Genre: Action / Adventure / Sci-Fi
  • IMDb Rating: 7.4/10

Synopsis

After being bitten by a genetically modified spider, shy teenager Peter Parker gains spider-like abilities that he eventually must use to fight evil as a superhero after tragedy befalls his family. He faces off against the Green Goblin while trying to balance his personal life and his feelings for Mary Jane Watson.
