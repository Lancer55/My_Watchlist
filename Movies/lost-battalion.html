The Lost Battalion

"We're giving them hell, and we're not leaving."

Movie Info

  • Director: Russell Mulcahy
  • Original Air Date: 2001
  • Genre: War / Action / Drama / History
  • Runtime: 1h 32m

Synopsis

Based on a true story from World War I, the film follows Major Charles Whittlesey and over 500 soldiers of the 77th Division who are cut off and surrounded by German forces in the Argonne Forest. Refusing to surrender despite running out of food, water, and ammunition, the "Lost Battalion" must endure several days of relentless attacks and friendly fire while waiting for a breakthrough.
