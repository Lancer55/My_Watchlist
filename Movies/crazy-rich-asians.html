Crazy Rich Asians

"The only thing crazier than love is family."

Movie Info

  • Director: Jon M. Chu
  • Release Year: 2018
  • Genre: Romance / Comedy
  • IMDb Rating: 6.9/10

Synopsis

New Yorker Rachel Chu accompanies her longtime boyfriend, Nick Young, to his best friend's wedding in Singapore. Excited about visiting Asia for the first time but nervous about meeting Nick's family, Rachel is unprepared to learn that Nick has neglected to mention a few key details about his life: he is the scion of one of the country's wealthiest families and one of its most eligible bachelors.
