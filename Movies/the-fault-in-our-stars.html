The Fault in Our Stars

"Some infinities are bigger than other infinities."

Movie Info

  • Director: Josh Boone
  • Release Year: 2014
  • Genre: Romance / Drama
  • IMDb Rating: 7.7/10

Synopsis

Hazel Grace Lancaster, a sixteen-year-old cancer patient, meets and falls in love with Gus Waters, a similarly afflicted teen from her cancer support group. Together, they embark on an affirming journey to visit a reclusive author in Amsterdam, learning that while life isn't perfect, love can be extraordinary.
