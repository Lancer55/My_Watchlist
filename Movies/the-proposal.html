The Proposal

"Here comes the bribe."

Movie Info

  • Director: Anne Fletcher
  • Release Year: 2009
  • Genre: Romance / Comedy
  • IMDb Rating: 6.8/10

Synopsis

Margaret Tate is a high-powered New York book editor who faces deportation to her native Canada because her visa renewal application was denied. In a moment of desperation, she claims she is engaged to her long-suffering assistant, Andrew Paxton. Andrew agrees to the charade but imposes his own conditions, including a trip to his hometown in Alaska to meet his quirky family. Under the watchful eye of a suspicious immigration official, the mismatched pair must maintain the ruse, leading to unexpected feelings.
