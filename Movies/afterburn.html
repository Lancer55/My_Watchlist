Synopsis

Set ten years after a solar flare destroyed all technology on Earth, a treasure hunter named Jake is hired to recover valuable art from the ruins of Europe. Amidst the lawless wasteland, he must navigate dangerous factions and old enemies to secure the artifacts before they fall into the wrong hands.
