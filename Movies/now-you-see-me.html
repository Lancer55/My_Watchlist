Now You See Me

"The closer you look, the less you'll see."

Movie Info

  • Director: Louis Leterrier
  • Original Air Date: 2013
  • Genre: Crime / Mystery / Thriller
  • Runtime: 1h 55m

Synopsis

An FBI agent and an Interpol detective track a team of illusionists known as "The Four Horsemen." The group pulls off a series of daring bank robberies during their performances, rewarding their audiences with the stolen money while staying one step ahead of the law in a world where nothing is as it seems.
