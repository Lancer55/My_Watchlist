Synopsis

Nikita Roy and The Book of Darkness is a 2025 mystery thriller that marks the directorial debut of Kussh S. Sinha. The film follows Nikita Roy (Sonakshi Sinha) as she becomes entangled in a web of supernatural occurrences and dark secrets after discovering a mysterious book. The story explores her descent into an unfolding nightmare where the lines between reality and fiction blur.
