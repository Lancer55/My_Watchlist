No Mercy

"How easy it is to hate, and how hard it is to forgive."

Movie Info

  • Director: Kim Hyeong-jun
  • Release Year: 2010
  • Genre: Action / Crime / Thriller
  • IMDb Rating: 7.4/10

Synopsis

A top forensic pathologist, Kang Min-ho, is about to retire to spend time with his returning daughter. However, he is pulled into one last case involving a dismembered corpse. When his daughter is kidnapped by the primary suspect, an environmental activist named Lee Sung-ho, Kang is forced to tamper with autopsy evidence to save her. What follows is a brutal and psychological game of revenge with a shocking conclusion.
