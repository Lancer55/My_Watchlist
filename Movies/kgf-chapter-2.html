Synopsis

K.G.F: Chapter 2 is the 2022 sequel to the 2018 film. The blood-soaked land of Kolar Gold Fields (KGF) now has a new overlord—Rocky, whose name strikes fear in the heart of his foes. While his allies look up to him as their savior, the government sees him as a threat to law and order; Rocky must battle threats from all sides for unchallenged supremacy.
