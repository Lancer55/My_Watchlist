Miracle in Cell No. 7

"Gift of 1997... Sailor Moon!"

Movie Info

  • Director: Lee Hwan-kyung
  • Original Air Date: 2013
  • Genre: Drama / Family
  • Runtime: 2h 7m

Synopsis

A mentally challenged father is wrongfully imprisoned for a crime he did not commit. Despite being held in a maximum-security prison, his fellow inmates in Cell No. 7—touched by his innocence and kindness—help him smuggle his young daughter inside. Together, they create a heartwarming world within the prison walls, proving that love can thrive in the most unlikely places.
