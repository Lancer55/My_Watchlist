Focus

"Never lose focus."

Movie Info

  • Director: Glenn Ficarra, John Requa
  • Release Year: 2015
  • Genre: Crime / Drama / Romance
  • IMDb Rating: 6.6/10

Synopsis

Nicky Spurgeon is an extremely accomplished con man who takes an amateur con artist, Jess, under his wing. He teaches her the tricks of the trade, but they become romantically involved, leading Nicky to break it off. Three years later, Jess—now an accomplished femme fatale—shows up in Buenos Aires, throwing Nicky's latest, dangerous scheme into disarray.
