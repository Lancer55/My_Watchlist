Synopsis

K.G.F: Chapter 1 is a 2018 Indian Kannada-language period action film. The story centers on Rocky, a high-ranking assassin in Mumbai who was born in poverty. To fulfill a promise to his dying mother, he seeks power and wealth, leading him to the Kolar Gold Fields, where he must liberate oppressed laborers while facing off against a brutal criminal empire.
